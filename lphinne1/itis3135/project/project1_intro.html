Project 1

Overview

This webpage serves as the hub for the BLNC brand. This is not a shop page, rather it's the artistic hub for BLNCs creators to showcase their work and stay connected with their community. My goal for this project is to make it easy for them to continuously update the page and maintain it without me, even with their limited web development knowledge.

The intended users of this website are mainly BLNCs community focused around clothing, other lifestyle interests, and the creators themselves. Users discovering the brand and website for the first time are also central to the design.

The website will feature an image gallery, meet the creators page, mission statement, news section, and contact page. In addition, links to BLNCs social media and other projects will be included in the footer.

Page Design

Image Gallery

  • Purpose | The gallery's main purpose is to be a location where the clients can upload some of the hundreds of images they take. It will serve as a collection and repository of the best images they choose.
  • Audience | The intended audience of the page is not just people seeking out this page specifically. This page will have to be designed with google images and other search engines in mind.
  • Content | Either in collections or standing alone by themselves, the images will be the only visible thing on this page. Images will take up as much space as possible to display the full content.
  • User Data Entry | This page will not require users to enter data.
  • Buttons / Hyperlinks | Clicking on an image will open up a subpage where the image can be displayed in full screen as well as offer some other actions.
  • Actions | After opening an image, users can view a magnified version of the image, download the image, leave a like on the image, share it with others, and a link to the creator that took the image.

News

  • Purpose | The news page's purpose is to give BLNCs creators a space to post announcements for upcoming events, releases, or any other updates.
  • Audience | The people following this page and coming back are likely dedicated fans of the brand and people truly interested in staying up to date with what they are doing.
  • Content | The content of this page is focused on longer text form articles. The page will show all the different posts in chronological order.
  • User Data Entry | This page will not require users to enter data.
  • Buttons / Hyperlinks | Hyperlinks on this page will be similar to the image gallery. Clicking on article headers will load up full versions of the articles which are easier to interact with.
  • Actions | The only action to do on these pages is going to be sharing the posts.

Mission Statement

  • Purpose | The purpose of the mission statement page is to detail BLNCs purpose beyond just a company.
  • Audience | The viewers of this page are customers that want to know more about BLNC.
  • Content | The content of this page is simply a list item displaying all of the clients provided mission statements.
  • User Data Entry | This page will not require users to enter data.
  • Buttons / Hyperlinks | This page will not contain any additional hyperlinks or buttons.
  • Actions | This page will not contain any additional actions.

Meet the Creators

  • Purpose | The meet the creators page's goal is to showcase the team behind BLNC and their personalities. This page will break prevalent theme more than any other page
  • Audience | The audience of this page is fans of BLNC or anybody interested in seeing who's behind the scenes.
  • Content | This page displays all the members of BLNC along with short biographies and more information about them as well as all of their relevant links. The styling will be catered to the liking of them.
  • User Data Entry | This page will not require users to enter data.
  • Buttons / Hyperlinks | The only hyperlinks on this page will be to the creators social media links.
  • Actions | Actions on the page include the various links to social media and creators other works.

Contact Us

  • Purpose | This page provides a space for users to directly contact the client and select various different reasons.
  • Audience | The audience of this page is anybody with a complaint, business idea, or any other reason to contact BLNC.
  • Content | A form will make up the content of this page, providing spaces to input different information such as text inputs and drop down boxes.
  • User Data Entry | This page will require users to enter data.
  • Data Validation | The data will need validation. The users email or phone number for example.
  • Buttons / Hyperlinks | Buttons for form submission and resetting will be the only buttons on the page.
  • Actions | As stated above, form submission and resetting will be the only actions on the page.

Dynamic Functionality

Image Gallery

The image gallery will feature javascript heavily. Generating all subpages for clicking on an image, magnifying that image, downloading it, and other features will all require some javascript.

News

The news page will feature javascript in the styling of headers and links. The page will have to by dynamic to support constant updates.

Mission Statement

This page will not feature any javascript.

Meet the Creators

This page will not feature any javascript.

Contact us

This page will need javascript in working with the form results and making sure the submissions are visible to the client. This page will also need form verification to make sure correct values are being entered.
